Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike?

Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your bike—not just “riding” your bike. This will help you be a more focused and legally compliant bike rider.

How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one?

Helmets are essential for your safety while engaging in activities like cycling, skateboarding, motorcycling, or any other activity that requires head protection. Over time, helmets can degrade, compromising their effectiveness in protecting your head.

My bike has been in storage is it safe to ride?

The safety of riding a bike that has been in storage depends on several factors, including how it was stored, for how long, and its condition when you take it out of storage.

What rules should I follow when riding my bike?

When riding your bike, it's essential to follow certain rules and safety guidelines to protect yourself and others on the road. These rules may vary depending on your location and local regulations.
